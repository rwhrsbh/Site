Frequently Asked Questions

What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a service that encrypts your internet connection and hides your online activity from prying eyes.

How do I set up the VPN?

After subscribing, you'll receive instructions to download our app. Install it, log in with your credentials, and connect to a server of your choice.

Can I use the VPN on multiple devices?

Yes, our plans allow you to use the VPN on multiple devices simultaneously. Check your plan details for the exact number of devices allowed.

Is my data fully secure with the VPN?

Yes, we use top-tier encryption protocols to ensure your data is secure. However, always use caution online and avoid visiting suspicious websites.

Will using a VPN affect my internet speed?

VPNs may slightly reduce your connection speed due to encryption, but we optimize our servers to minimize the impact. Switching servers can help if you experience slowdowns.

Which VPN server should I choose?

The best server depends on your location and needs. Choose a server close to your location for optimal speed, or one in another country to bypass geo-restrictions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your account settings. If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact our support team.

Having connection issues?

Restart the VPN app

Switch to a different server

Check your internet connection

Update the VPN app to the latest version

If problems persist, contact our support team

Troubleshooting FAQs

Why is my VPN connection dropping?

This can happen due to network instability or server overload. Try switching to a less crowded server or reconnecting your internet.

What if I cannot access certain websites?

Some websites actively block VPN traffic. Try using different servers or contact our support team for assistance in bypassing these blocks.
